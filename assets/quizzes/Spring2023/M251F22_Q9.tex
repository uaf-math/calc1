\problem{10} On a given day, the flow rate $F$, measured in cars per hour, on a congested roadway is given by $\displaystyle F(v)=\frac{5v}{{v^2}+100}$ where $v$ is the speed of the traffic in miles per hour.
\begin{subproblems}
	\item Given the context of the problem, what is a reasonable domain?
	\vspace{0.5in}
	\item Find all critical numbers of the $F(v)$ in your domain in part (a). 
	\vfill
	\item \textbf{Use Calculus} to determine if the critical number(s) correspond to an absolute minimum or absolute maximum or neither. Note providing organized clear work here is crucial.
	\vfill
	\item Write a complete sentence explaining what your work in part (c) indicates about traffic flow.
	
	
	\vspace{0.5in}
\end{subproblems}
